Instructions for adding a Product

1.You have to choose a category for your product

2.The category must have a category ID specified by our system

3.Currently there are 7 categories for your product

4.You can choose from the specified category

List of categories:

  1. Babies and Toys => (B&T)
  2. Beverages => (Bev)
  3. Electronics & Accessories => (EA)
  4. Groceries => (GF)
  5. Health and Beauty => (HAB)
  6. Kitchen Appliances => (KApp)
  7. Sports and Outdoors => (Sp&O)

5.While adding products, the category of the product must be one of the above

6.You should enter the category code specified above, not the category's name itself

7.If no category matches your product, you can specify it as "other"
